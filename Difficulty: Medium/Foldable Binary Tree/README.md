<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/foldable-binary-tree/1">Foldable Binary Tree</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p class="PDq2pG_selectionAnchorContainer" data-start="126" data-end="202"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">A binary tree is said to be foldable if its left and right subtrees are <strong data-start="283" data-end="341">mirror images </strong>of each other in terms of<strong data-start="283" data-end="341"> structure only</strong> (Values stored in the nodes are not considered). </span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Given the root of a binary tree, find if the tree is foldable.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Note:</strong> An empty tree is also considered foldable.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>root[] = [10, 7, 15, N, 9, 11, N]<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/930746/Web/Other/blobid0_1783944431.png" width="250" height="156"> <br><strong>Output: </strong>true
<strong>Explanation</strong>: The left and right subtrees are mirror images of each other in terms of structure. Node 7 has no left child and a right child (9). The corresponding mirrored node 15 has a left child (11) and no right child. Since every node in the left subtree has a corresponding mirrored node in the right subtree, the tree is foldable.</span>
</pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>root[] = [10, 7, 15, 5, N, 11, N]<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/930746/Web/Other/blobid1_1783944462.png" width="250" height="156"> <br><strong>Output: </strong>false
<strong>Explanation</strong>: The left and right subtrees are not mirror images of each other in terms of structure. Node 7 has a left child (5) and no right child, whereas its mirrored node 15 has a left child (11) and no right child. For the tree to be foldable, 7's left child should correspond to 15's right child. Since this condition is not satisfied, the tree is not foldable.</span>
</pre>
